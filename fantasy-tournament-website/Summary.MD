## üí∞ Wallet System Business Logic

### Deposit System
1. **User Deposits Money** ‚Üí **Wallet Balance**
2. **Wallet Balance** ‚Üí **Used for Tournament Entry Fees**
3. **Entry Fees** ‚Üí **Deducted from Wallet Balance**

### Withdrawal System
1. **Admin Awards Prize** ‚Üí **Winnings Balance**
2. **Winnings Balance** ‚Üí **Available for Withdrawal**
3. **Users Cannot Withdraw** ‚Üí **Original Deposits (Wallet Balance)**

### Transaction Flow
```
Deposit ‚Üí Wallet Balance ‚Üí Tournament Entry ‚Üí Prize Award ‚Üí Winnings Balance ‚Üí Withdrawal
```

This system ensures users can only withdraw money they've won, not their original deposits, providing platform revenue through entry fees while maintaining fair prize distribution.
